Step 2 — Three Pillars Check

Which pillars does your solution use? Strong GenAI products combine multiple pillars — single-pillar solutions are vulnerable to the ChatGPT Tax.

✍️
Generate
Creates new content: text, images, code, summaries, translations
🔍
Retrieve
Searches, filters, or surfaces relevant information from data sources
⚡
Act
Takes actions: API calls, database writes, workflow triggers, notifications
⚠️ Single-pillar solutions are vulnerable to the ChatGPT Tax. Users can replicate "generate-only" or "retrieve-only" features with a €20/month ChatGPT subscription. Consider adding more pillars.

Step 4 — Cornerstone of Success

What gives your product a defensible advantage? Check all that apply. Without at least 2 cornerstones, you'll struggle to beat "just use ChatGPT."

✓
Proprietary Data
Access to unique datasets competitors can't easily replicate
✓
Specialized Behavior
Fine-tuned models, custom prompts, or domain expertise baked in
✓
Workflow Integration
Embedded in existing tools (Slack, CRM, ERP) — not a standalone app
✓
Privacy Advantage
On-premise, data residency, or compliance features competitors lack
⚠️ Weak defensibility. With fewer than 2 cornerstones, you'll struggle to beat "just use ChatGPT." Consider strengthening your moat before building.
